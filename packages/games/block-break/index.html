Instructions

  • Move Left or Right using the Keyboard or Mouse to ensure the ball hits the paddle.
  • Click on screen or press Spacebar to pause game.
  • Clear all blocks by hitting them with the ball to win.
  • Miss the ball with your paddle & lose a live
  • When you lose all your lives, it's game over.
  • Wait for game to reload after lossing / winning.
